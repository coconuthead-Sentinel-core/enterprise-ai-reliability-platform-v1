Data Governance and Privacy Plan
Version: 2.0
LLM Scope Statement
This artifact applies to large language model (LLM) product engineering, evaluation, release governance, and operational reliability in 2026 enterprise environments.


1. Governance Objectives
- Ensure data integrity, lineage, and accountability.
- Protect sensitive information across ingestion, storage, and export.
- Provide audit-ready retention and legal hold controls.

2. Data Classification
- Public
- Internal
- Confidential
- Restricted

3. Privacy Controls
- Data minimization by default
- Purpose limitation for collected fields
- Access logging for all sensitive data operations
- Pseudonymization where feasible

4. Retention Policy
- Operational telemetry: 12 months
- Gate decisions and audit logs: 7 years
- Security findings: 5 years minimum
- Legal hold overrides default retention when active

5. Access Control
- Role-based authorization with least privilege
- Break-glass access requires dual approval and post-access audit

6. Data Subject and Regulatory Response
- Establish request workflow for data access and deletion where applicable
- Maintain regulator-ready evidence package with timestamped controls

7. Compliance Note
This plan is designed for legal and regulatory readiness. Jurisdiction-specific legal determinations require licensed counsel.

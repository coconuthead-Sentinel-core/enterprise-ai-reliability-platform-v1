Project Charter
Project: Enterprise AI Reliability Platform (EARP)
Version: 2.0
LLM Scope Statement
This artifact applies to large language model (LLM) product engineering, evaluation, release governance, and operational reliability in 2026 enterprise environments.

Date: 2026-04-14

1. Project Objective
Design and launch an enterprise software platform that quantifies AI system reliability and provides auditable release decisions for production environments.

2. Problem Statement
Organizations deploy AI features without consistent reliability evidence, resulting in regression risk, governance failures, and delayed releases.

3. Solution Summary
EARP provides a reliability control plane with policy-based release gates, evidence traceability, and dashboard-driven decision support.

4. Stakeholders
- Executive Sponsor
- Product Manager
- Engineering Lead
- Security Lead
- Legal and Compliance Lead
- Operations Lead
- Customer Success Lead

5. In-Scope Capabilities
- Reliability score computation
- Evidence ingestion and normalization
- Policy-driven gate evaluation
- Audit trail generation
- Incident and remediation workflows

6. Out-of-Scope
- Model training pipeline development
- Non-enterprise consumer social features
- Jurisdiction-specific legal opinions

7. Success Criteria
- Reduction in failed production releases
- Reduced mean time to detect reliability regressions
- Full traceability from requirement to release decision
- Documented compliance and governance evidence

8. Inclusivity and Professional Conduct Requirements
The product and documentation must be culturally neutral, non-discriminatory, and suitable for users from all religious and non-religious backgrounds.

9. Governance and Approval Workflow
Document set approval order:
1) Product and Architecture Review
2) Security and Compliance Review
3) Legal Readiness Review
4) Executive Go Decision

10. Ownership
The project sponsor owns the product concept, funded delivery, and resulting intellectual property rights subject to contractor agreements and applicable law.

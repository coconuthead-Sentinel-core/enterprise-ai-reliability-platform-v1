Building Block View
Version: 2.0
LLM Scope Statement
This artifact applies to large language model (LLM) product engineering, evaluation, release governance, and operational reliability in 2026 enterprise environments.


Level 1: Platform Context
- External Connectors
- EARP Control Plane
- Enterprise Users and Reviewers

Level 2: Primary Components
- Connector Gateway
- Evidence Registry
- Scoring Engine
- Policy Engine
- Reporting and Export Module
- Identity and Access Module

Level 3: Internal Responsibilities
Connector Gateway: schema validation and source authentication.
Evidence Registry: canonical entities, versioning, and lineage.
Scoring Engine: metric aggregation and weighted computation.
Policy Engine: rule execution and gate adjudication.
Reporting Module: evidence packaging and export generation.
IAM Module: RBAC, approval constraints, and audit ownership.

Quality Attributes Mapped
- Reliability: resilient ingest and deterministic evaluation
- Security: strong authentication and least privilege
- Maintainability: bounded interfaces and contract tests
- Auditability: immutable event and decision logs

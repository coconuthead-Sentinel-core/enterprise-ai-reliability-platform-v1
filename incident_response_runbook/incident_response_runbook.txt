Incident Response Runbook
Version: 2.0
LLM Scope Statement
This artifact applies to large language model (LLM) product engineering, evaluation, release governance, and operational reliability in 2026 enterprise environments.


1. Severity Model
SEV-1 Critical business disruption
SEV-2 Major functional degradation
SEV-3 Localized functional issue
SEV-4 Minor non-blocking issue

2. Response Workflow
Step 1: Detect and classify incident severity.
Step 2: Assign incident commander and responders.
Step 3: Stabilize service and communicate status.
Step 4: Identify root cause and apply remediation.
Step 5: Validate recovery and close incident.
Step 6: Publish post-incident report with corrective actions.

3. Communication Standards
- Internal status updates every 30 minutes for SEV-1/SEV-2
- Executive summary for incidents with customer impact

4. Evidence Requirements
- Timeline log
- Affected components
- User impact analysis
- Mitigation and verification records

5. Postmortem Requirements
Root cause category, contributing factors, prevention tasks, and owner commitments with due dates.

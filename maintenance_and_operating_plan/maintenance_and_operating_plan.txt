Maintenance and Operating Plan
Version: 2.0
LLM Scope Statement
This artifact applies to large language model (LLM) product engineering, evaluation, release governance, and operational reliability in 2026 enterprise environments.


1. Operating Objectives
Maintain reliability, security, and compliance posture after general availability.

2. Routine Operations
- Daily connector health review
- Weekly policy effectiveness review
- Monthly capacity and cost review
- Quarterly access and control recertification

3. Change Management
- All production changes require approved change request
- High-risk changes require staged deployment and rollback rehearsal

4. Support Model
- Tier 1: operational triage
- Tier 2: platform engineering support
- Tier 3: architecture and vendor escalation

5. Continuous Improvement
- Feed incident learnings into backlog
- Recalibrate gate thresholds using measured outcomes
- Review KPI trends for systemic quality improvement

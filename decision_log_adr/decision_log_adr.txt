LLM Scope Statement
This artifact applies to large language model (LLM) product engineering, evaluation, release governance, and operational reliability in 2026 enterprise environments.


ADR-001: API-First Contract Governance
Status: Accepted
Decision: OpenAPI contract must be approved before implementation.
Rationale: Reduces integration defects and improves parallel development.

ADR-002: Deterministic Policy Evaluation
Status: Accepted
Decision: Gate rules execute through deterministic evaluation with versioned policies.
Rationale: Ensures repeatability and audit defensibility.

ADR-003: Immutable Decision Ledger
Status: Accepted
Decision: Decision outputs stored in append-only audit records.
Rationale: Supports compliance evidence and forensic analysis.

ADR-004: Role-Based Approval Separation
Status: Accepted
Decision: No single role may author, approve, and release the same gate decision.
Rationale: Reduces control failure risk.

ADR-005: Inclusive Enterprise Language Standard
Status: Accepted
Decision: User-facing wording must remain culturally neutral and professional.
Rationale: Required for global enterprise adoption and non-discrimination compliance.

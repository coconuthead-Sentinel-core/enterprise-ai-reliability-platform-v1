Product Vision Statement
Version: 2.0
LLM Scope Statement
This artifact applies to large language model (LLM) product engineering, evaluation, release governance, and operational reliability in 2026 enterprise environments.


Vision
EARP is a reliability engineering platform that enables teams to release AI-enabled software with measurable confidence, auditable controls, and repeatable governance.

Design Principles
1. Reliability First: every release decision must be evidence-backed.
2. Transparency by Default: each score must include explainable factors.
3. Policy as Code: governance rules are encoded, testable, and versioned.
4. Inclusive Enterprise UX: language and workflows are neutral and respectful for all users.
5. Operational Accountability: ownership and response procedures are explicit.

Target Users
- Engineering managers
- Site reliability engineers
- AI platform teams
- Security and compliance analysts
- Release managers

Vision Outcomes
- Faster release cycles with lower risk
- Fewer governance exceptions
- Higher trust in AI production behavior
- Better executive visibility into operational readiness

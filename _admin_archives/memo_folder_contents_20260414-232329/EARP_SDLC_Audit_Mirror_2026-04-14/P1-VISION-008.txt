P1-VISION-008
EARP Project Vision
Version: 1.1
Date: 2026-04-14

1) Vision Statement
EARP is an enterprise reliability platform that unifies release governance, evidence quality, and operational confidence in a clear and inclusive user experience.

2) Mission
Provide a dependable, audit-ready reliability system that organizations can use daily without cultural or religious friction.

3) Product Principles
- Reliability first: decisions are traceable and evidence-backed.
- Safety first: high-risk states trigger protective controls.
- Inclusion first: user-facing language and behavior remain culturally neutral.
- Transparency first: approval and override events are auditable.

4) Inclusive Use Commitment
The platform is designed for global teams and must be safe and welcoming for users from all cultural, ethnic, and religious backgrounds, including users who prefer non-religious contexts.

5) Readiness Commitment
No coding begins until this vision and related documentation pass formal proof-check.
